# SEO Indexing Guide for Langzy

## Why Your Site Might Not Be Showing in Google Search

### Common Reasons:
1. **New Website**: Google takes time to discover and index new sites (can take days to weeks)
2. **Not Submitted**: Google hasn't been told about your site yet
3. **Low Authority**: New sites have low domain authority, so they rank lower initially
4. **Competition**: "Langzy" might be competing with other results

## ‚úÖ Immediate Actions to Get Indexed

### 1. Submit to Google Search Console (CRITICAL)
**Steps:**
1. Go to https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Click "Add Property"
3. Enter your domain: `https://langzy.co`
4. Verify ownership (choose one method):
   - HTML file upload
   - HTML tag (add to your homepage)
   - DNS record
   - Google Analytics
5. Once verified, go to "Sitemaps" in the left menu
6. Enter: `https://langzy.co/sitemap.xml`
7. Click "Submit"

### 2. Request Indexing for Key Pages
**In Google Search Console:**
1. Use the URL Inspection tool
2. Enter each important URL:
   - https://langzy.co
   - https://langzy.co/our-team
   - https://langzy.co/about-us
   - https://langzy.co/faq
   - https://langzy.co/contact
3. Click "Request Indexing" for each

### 3. Submit Sitemap to Other Search Engines
- **Bing Webmaster Tools**: https://www.bing.com/webmasters
- **Yandex Webmaster**: https://webmaster.yandex.com

### 4. Create Backlinks (Get Other Sites to Link to You)
- Social media profiles (Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter)
- Business directories
- Educational platforms
- Partner websites
- Guest posts on education blogs

### 5. Share on Social Media
- Post about Langzy on all your social channels
- Include the website URL
- Google crawls social media links

### 6. Create Google Business Profile (If Applicable)
- If you have a physical location, create a Google Business Profile
- This helps with local SEO
